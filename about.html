What do I do when I'm free

I really enjoy watching anime, movies and tv shows, and playing videogames. I recently started reading as well and my favorite novel is undoubtedly Gorge Orwell's 1984. Math (especially discrete math and probability) and puzzles are two things I really enjoy spending hours on, if I'm able to find time. Music is something I can't live without, I can forget to carry clothes while travelling but never my headphones. I love listening to rock, classical piano and almost any genre, I guess. Some of my favourite artists include Coldplay, Eminem, Linkin Park, Ed Sheeran and the list goes on. Also, I don't think I need to tell you that technology is an inseparable part of my life. A wise person once said "You're not a true engineer if you're not crazy about tech and gadgets" and as you guessed it, that wise person is obviously me.

How I fell in love with Machine Learning

Just like any other engineering student I heard the buzz words machine learning and artificial intelligence. I decided to give it a try and in the process of educating myself I came across deep learning and reinforcement learning, and was blown away by what it's capable of doing with nothing but simple math (well... not exactly simple, but many simple concepts combined which makes it seem complex). Watching your code beating a game or doing complex tasks and to know mechanics behind why and how it works, is extremely satisfying.
